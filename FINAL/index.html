About Bryant

My name is Bryant Colon, and I am currently entering my sophomore year at Michigan State University in the Media and Information program within the College of Communication Arts and Sciences. I am very much a music person, as I have been playing guitar for about ten years, drumming for over fifteen years, and I collect CDs and vinyl LPs. Furthermore, I am a DJ at Michigan State's student radio station, as well as do production and engineering work there as well.
