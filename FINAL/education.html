Education Information

I graduated from high school in May of 2012 from a school called Kenowa Hills, which is in the suburbs of Grand Rapids, Michigan. It was here I first encountered web development, as well as 3D modeling with Autodesk AutoCAD and DS Solidworks, I had even won awards for some of my CAD work. I now attend Michgian State University and am majoring in Media and Information. My concentration area is not yet decided, but will most likely be TV, Cinema, and Radio. As far as my radio experience goes, I have gained quite a bit of it from volunteering and working at the University's student radio station, Impact 89fm. Here I DJ two shifts a week, am an audio engineer for our radio show with City Pulse newspaper, and I audio produce promos and public service announcements for the station, which involves writing scripts and sound designing with Avid Pro Tools.
